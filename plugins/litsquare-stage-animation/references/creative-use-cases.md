# Creative Use Cases

- Social launch videos: square, portrait feed, and vertical cutdowns from one source.
- Movie theater pre-roll: DCI 4K or 8K titles with slow camera motion and high sample-count motion blur.
- Weather channel loops: API-driven regional forecast packs with maps, icons, crawls, and lower thirds.
- Sports graphics: automated score, player stat, matchup, and highlight-card renders.
- Finance explainers: market open/close summaries, charts, tickers, and portfolio recaps.
- Product launches: 3D product orbit, typography reveal, feature callouts, and localized variants.
- Event signage: LED wall loops, sponsor rotations, countdowns, and wayfinding motion.
- Museum and gallery walls: data-driven timelines, generative maps, and projection-ready loops.
- Retail screens: inventory-aware product cards and regional promotions.
- Real-estate listing videos: property facts, maps, image sequences, and agent branding.
- Education explainers: procedural diagrams and whiteboard-style motion.
- Music visuals: audio-reactive typography, waveform scenes, and album teasers.
- News packages: lower thirds, charts, transitions, breaking-news shells, and explainer cards.
- Personalized ads: generated variants by city, language, product, or audience segment.
